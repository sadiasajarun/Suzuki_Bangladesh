The fine print

Policies & schedules.

Maintenance schedule

For all Suzuki motorbikes registered in Bangladesh, follow the schedule below to keep performance optimal and your warranty intact:

  • 1st service — 500 km or 30 days (free)
  • 2nd service — 2,000 km or 90 days (free)
  • 3rd service — 4,000 km or 180 days (free)
  • 4th service onward — every 3,000 km or 6 months (paid · standard catalogue rates)
  • Air filter, spark plug, and engine oil are interval-based — they don't always coincide with the calendar service.
Warranty policy

Every new Suzuki sold by Rancon Motor Bikes Ltd carries a 2-year / 30,000 km standard warranty — whichever comes first.

What's covered: manufacturing defects in the engine, transmission, electrical system, frame, and bodywork.

What's not: consumables (oil, filters, tyres, brake pads, chain), accident damage, modifications, and damage from skipped service intervals or non-genuine parts.

Your warranty stays valid only if all scheduled services are performed at an authorised Suzuki dealer with genuine parts. To claim, present your warranty book and service log at any authorised dealer or call 16638.

Free vs paid service checkpoints

Free services (1st, 2nd, 3rd) cover labour for the standard checklist — you pay only for engine oil and any consumables that need replacing.

Paid services from the 4th onward are billed at standard catalogue rates — labour plus parts. Your dealer will walk you through what your bike needs at each interval before any work begins.

Genuine parts (filters, oil, plugs) and any unforeseen replacements are itemised separately on every invoice so there are no surprises.

Pre-ride check (free, 5 minutes)

Heading on a long trip? Drop by any Suzuki dealer for a free pre-ride check — no booking needed.

  • Tyre pressure & tread
  • Brake fluid & brake pad wear
  • Lights, horn, indicators
  • Drive chain tension & lubrication
  • Fluid leak inspection
  • Battery voltage

Takes about 5 minutes. We'll flag anything that needs attention before you ride.
