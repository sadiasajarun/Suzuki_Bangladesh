Common questions

Before you twist the throttle.

Is the test ride free?
Yes — every test ride at an authorised Suzuki Bangladesh dealer is free of charge, with no obligation to buy.
What do I need to bring?
A valid Bangladesh driving licence (motorcycle category), one national ID document (NID, passport, or driving licence is fine), and ideally closed-toe shoes. The dealer will provide a helmet and gloves.
Can I bring a pillion?
For the test ride itself, no — for safety and insurance reasons we keep the test rider solo. Your pillion is welcome at the dealer to inspect the bike with you before and after the ride.
What if I love the bike?
The dealer can guide you through paperwork, financing options, and on-the-road delivery the same day for in-stock models. There's no pressure — many of our riders book two or three test rides before deciding.
What if I'm late?
No problem — give the dealer a call (their direct number will be in your confirmation) and we'll do our best to slot you in. Try to give us at least 30 minutes' notice if possible.
