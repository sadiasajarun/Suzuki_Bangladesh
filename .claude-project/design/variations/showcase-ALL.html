How to confirm your selection

To advance P3, the orchestrator needs approved: true recorded in DESIGN_STATUS.md. Tell the orchestrator your pick (A, B, C, or a Mix specifying which elements from each), and it will write the file and continue P3e (full 8-page generation).

Example structure that will be written to .claude-project/design/DESIGN_STATUS.md:

# DESIGN_STATUS.md (P3d output)
selected_variation: B          # A | B | C | Mix
approved: true
approved_at: "2026-04-30T..."
approved_by: "Sajarun Sadia (PM)"
notes: |
  Client confirmed Variation B during review meeting.
  Mix overrides: keep B's hero + Section 4 environment cycling,
  but adopt C's cream-section break for Bangladesh Pride.
